Chapter 1: Ein Ruhiger Ort

Cochem Township was a rather quiet place. A not too big place with a populus of 5000. Nothing too climatic as generally it was just a normal day. But that’s where this starts. A lot of people in Germany were still recovering from 1945 and years before as it was a never-ending nightmare for them.        

The USA was cooking something up for Germany afterwards though. They had become friends but they had a secret operation. “The Road To The West: OPERATION” or rather, “Die Straße nach Westen: BETRIEB.” The Road To The west was a very complex operative plan.

The USA had Britain and France on their side to go offense on Germany, and the USA would secretly send troops to areas like Cochem Township. This was due to the USA still thinking Germany was over-run with Nazis.

Their game plan was simple. Go offense and blast all out. Very simple, isn't it? For Cochem Township, some strong people resided in it. When Telegram Transmissions leaked, all went wild, and that was no exception for our little quiet town of Cochem.
